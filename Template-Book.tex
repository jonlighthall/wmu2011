\chapter{Notes}
\begin{itemize}
	\item In the book class, fancyvrb and VerbatimFootnotes are needed for proper links to footnotes with hyperref.
	\item In the wmu-thesis class, (when compiling the thesis) the inclusion of VerbatimFootnotes causes errors with hyperref.
	\item The default vertical spacing in the book class is to place unnumbered chapter headings on the same level as the Table of Contents and list of tables, etc.
	\item The position of these levels moves down 0.4 baselineskip when double spacing is used.  Adding \\ \verb|\vspace{-0.4\baselineskip}| to the section headings or bracketing the declaration of the section headings with \verb|singlespace| and \verb|\doublespce| moves the section headings to their position with singlespace.
	
	\item the package tocloft raises the headings of the toc, lof, lot by 30\,pt (1.5 baselineskip)for 10\,pt, 1.25 baselineskip or 27.5\,pt for 11\,pt, 25\,pt or about 1 baseline skip for 12\,pt.  baseline skip is \the\baselineskip
\end{itemize}
